   Note and Usage

This is "Unofficial/Fan made Exp Calculator for DelugeRPG (up to 100M Exp) - A fork of Psyduck's Exp Calculator".

  1. Left Panel - Calculator and Right Panel - List of Wild Pokemon & Trainers Exp.

  2. Enter your Current Exp and your Target Exp. Note: It assumes you have already beaten all region gym leaders and that your pokemon is at level 100.

  3. It will shows solution button. You can battle any other trainers that give the same amount of exp as those in the suggested solution.

  4. If a solution is not automatically generated or Exp to target is not turn to 0, Add some exp from the trainer list on the right by clicking on them.

  5. You can click the name of the trainer and pokemon in right panel. It will shows a popover that will redirect to the trainers and map for wild pokemon.

  6. If you want to try other DelugeRPG Exp Calculator for DelugeRPG - visit Aihvz 100K Exp Calculator and Official Exp Calculator.

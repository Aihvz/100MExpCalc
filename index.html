   Note and Usage

This is "Unofficial/Fan made Exp Calculator for DelugeRPG (up to 150M Exp) - A fork of Psyduck's Exp Calculator ".

  1. Left Panel - Calculator and Right Panel - List of Wild Pokemon & Trainers EXP.

  2. Enter your Current EXP and your Target EXP. Note: Assumes you have already beaten all region gym leaders and that your pokemon is at level 100.

  3. It will shows solution button. You can battle any other trainers that give the same amount of EXP as those in the suggested solution.

  4. If a solution is not automatically generated or EXP to target is not turn to 0 then add some EXP from the trainer list on the right by clicking on them.

  5. In the solution, the three lines ( ) represent trainers who provide the same amount of experience.

  6. This calculator is designed for non-event Pokémon and calculates within the specified range(Current EXP to Target EXP)
    • 100,000 to 10 Million
    • 10 Million to 50 Million
    • 50 Million to 100 Million
    • 100 Million to 150 Million
